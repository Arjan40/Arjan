About Us

Pappu Samose Waley is a legendary street food vendor known for mouthwatering samosas that have become an integral part of Kanpur's culinary identity.

Since 1984, we have been serving the Kanpur community with love, passion, and authentic flavors. What started as a small stall on Birhana Road has now become a must-visit destination for food lovers across the city.

Our secret lies in the perfect blend of spices, high-quality ingredients, and traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. Each samosa is crafted with care, ensuring that every bite takes you on a flavorful journey.
